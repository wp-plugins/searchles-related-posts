Display contextually related posts from your blog using the Searchles Discovery Platform. Searchles technology is in use by such big media sites as The Washington Post and The DenverPost.


  1. Download the plugin to a local directory on your computer (or use the plugin finder to locate the plugin on WordPress' site)
  2. Go to your WordPress dashboard (where you edit your blog)
  3. On the left side, click on Plugins -> Add New
  4. Under "Install a plugin in .zip format" click "browse" and locate the file you downloaded
  5. Click "Install Now"
  6. It should say "Successfully installed the plugin" if all went correctly.
  7. Click on "Activate Plugin"
  8. Under Appearance on the left column, click "Widgets"
  9. Click "Add" next to Searchles Discovery
  10. Very Important: On the right side, click "Edit" to configure the widget, then click "Save Changes" (even if you don't change anything).
  11. Change the size and colors of widget to make it fit your layout (optional)
  12. Email us at widget -at- searchles dot com for help.
